About us

WELCOME TO OUR YOGA CLUB

Known for providing long-lasting emotional & psychological relief and treating all types of anxieties, clinical depressions and getting rid of stress, yoga also enhances your body.

In our club you will be able to practice, learn meditation and enhance your health in the most pleasant way.

Our philosophy

Yoga as a Lifestyle

Yoga has always been something more, than just a workout routine. The main things that yoga will help you to achieve are:

  • Relaxation after work
  • Much more peaceful state of mind
  • Refreshed body
  • Reenergized mind
  • Body flexibility
Our Classes

GROUP LESSONS

Yoga For Beginners

Yoga For Pregnant

Yoga will help you keep the body both strong and flexible in preparation for labour and birth.

YOGA CORE

In today’s busy urban lifestyle yoga is a great way to get rid of stress and balance your inner perception.

YOGA RESTORE

A very slow paced/still deeply relaxing class to completely support and restore the body, mind and spirit.
